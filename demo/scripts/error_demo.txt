VoxBank Error Handling Demo Script

Scenario 1: Insufficient Funds
User: "Transfer ₹100,000 to Priya"
System: "I'll help you transfer ₹100,000 to Priya."
[Processing...]
System: "I'm sorry, but you have insufficient funds. Your current balance is ₹50,000.00, which is less than the requested amount of ₹100,000. Would you like to transfer a different amount?"

Scenario 2: Invalid Recipient
User: "Send money to John"
System: "I couldn't find John in your contacts. Could you please provide the account number or phone number?"

Scenario 3: Network Error
User: "What's my balance?"
System: "I'm having trouble connecting to the banking system right now. Please try again in a moment."
